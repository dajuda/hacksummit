About

This game aims at teaching basic ASL (American Sign Language) lessons, using the human brain patter recognition. It has a architecture of easy scaling, in regards of animation and language, making it portable to any language in the world. We believe this project will be a small contribution to a better sociabilization of this small portion of society. Which can be excluded because of a mere physical problema that prevens voice communication.
